Privacy Policy

English Adventures - Educational Grammar Platform

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

English Adventures ("we," "us," "our," or "the Application") is an educational web-based learning platform designed to teach English grammar, specifically focusing on modal verbs. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

Learning Data: We store your progress, completed activities, scores, and achievements to provide personalized learning experiences.

Device Information: We may collect information about your browser, device type, and language preferences to optimize your experience.

No Personal Identification: We do not require you to create an account or submit personal information like your name, email, or age to use the Application.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To track your learning progress and adjust difficulty levels
  • To provide feedback and personalized educational recommendations
  • To improve the Application's functionality and user experience
  • To diagnose technical issues and maintain security

4. Data Storage & Security

Learning data is stored locally in your browser's local storage. We do not transmit your learning data to external servers unless you voluntarily share content. We implement reasonable security measures to protect technical infrastructure.

5. Third-Party Services

Unsplash: The Application uses images from Unsplash, an image library. Please refer to Unsplash's privacy policy for information about their data practices.

Font Awesome: Icons are sourced from Font Awesome, which has its own privacy policy.

p5.js: We use the p5.js library. Refer to p5.js documentation for their practices.

6. Cookies & Tracking

The Application uses browser local storage to remember your preferences and learning progress. We do not use cookies for advertising or external tracking.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The Application is designed for learners of all ages, including children under 13. Parents should be aware that learning progress is stored locally and no personal information is collected without explicit disclosure. Parental consent is recommended for young learners.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your learning data
  • Clear your progress by resetting data within the Application settings
  • Disable local storage through your browser settings
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Application evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Application indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
